Using AI to Decrease Latency

Latent Space reduces audio payloads by 78%, enabling musicians and others to synchronize as closely as possible.

Autoencoders

Autoencoders are like springs:
  1. they first compress the input,
  2. then modify the compressed version (called the latent space),
  3. and then stretch the latent space back into the output.
Latent Space uses recurrent neural autoencoders (RAEs) to optimize audio data transmission by domain—a novel approach to communication.
